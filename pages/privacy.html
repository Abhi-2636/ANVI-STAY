Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we collect, use, and protect your information.

ANVI STAY is committed to protecting the privacy of all residents and visitors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it safe. By using our services, you consent to the practices described below.

1

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information from residents:

  • Personal Details: Full name, date of birth, gender, and nationality.
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address, and emergency contact details.
  • Identity Documents: Aadhaar card, passport, visa details (for international students), and university ID.
  • Financial Information: UPI ID, bank account details (for refund processing), and payment transaction records.
  • Photographs: Profile photo and document scans uploaded during onboarding.
  • CCTV Footage: Video recordings from surveillance cameras installed at property premises.
2

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Resident identity verification and onboarding
  • Rent collection, payment tracking, and receipt generation
  • Maintenance request management and resolution
  • Communication via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or phone
  • Security and safety monitoring through CCTV
  • Legal compliance and dispute resolution
  • Improving our services and resident experience
3

Data Storage & Security

  • All personal data is stored securely using encrypted databases and access-controlled systems.
  • Only authorized personnel have access to resident information.
  • CCTV footage is retained for a maximum of 30 days unless required for an investigation.
  • Payment data is handled through secure, trusted payment gateways. We do not store full card or UPI PIN details.
  • Document uploads are stored with restricted access and are deleted within 90 days of check-out.
4

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only in these cases:

  • Legal Authorities: When mandated by law enforcement or government authorities.
  • University Verification: For academic status confirmation if required.
  • Payment Partners: UPI and banking partners for processing rent and refunds.
  • Emergency Contacts: In case of medical or safety emergencies.
5

Cookies & Website Tracking

  • Our website may use cookies and local storage for session management and user preferences (e.g., dark mode, login state).
  • We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.
  • Analytics data (page views, device type) may be collected anonymously to improve user experience.
6

Your Rights as a Resident

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data stored with us.
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data after check-out (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw Consent — Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
