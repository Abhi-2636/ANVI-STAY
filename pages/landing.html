Frequently Asked

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about staying with us

How far are the properties from LPU?

All our properties are located within 2-5 minutes of driving distance from LPU's main gate at Law Gate, Phagwara. Many students walk or use the university shuttle.

What is included in the rent?

Rent covers your furnished room, Wi-Fi, water supply, CCTV security, and housekeeping. Electricity and maintenance are charged separately based on usage at ₹13/unit + ₹300 maintenance.

Is there a rental agreement?

Yes, we provide a proper legal rental agreement for all tenants. The standard lease is for 6 months and can be renewed. A security deposit (usually 1-2 months rent) is required.

What are the timings and rules?

Gate closes by 10:00 – 10:30 PM depending on the property. Visitors are allowed till 7-8 PM. Smoking and loud music are not allowed inside rooms.

Is food available?

Some properties like Blessing PG include home-cooked meals. Others have common kitchen access and are close to the market area with many affordable food options and mess services.

How do I pay rent?

Rent can be paid via UPI/online transfer. You'll receive WhatsApp reminders and can track your payment status through the Resident Portal with a simple room number and password login.
