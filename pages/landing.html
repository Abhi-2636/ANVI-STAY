How far are the properties from LPU?

All our properties are located within 2-5 minutes of driving distance from LPU's main gate at Law Gate, Phagwara. Many students walk or use the easily available e-rickshaws.

What is included in the rent?

Rent covers your luxuriously furnished room, high-speed Wi-Fi, 24/7 water supply, advanced CCTV security, and professional housekeeping. Electricity and maintenance are practically logged and charged separately based on actual usage.

Is there a rental agreement?

Yes, we ensure complete transparency by providing a proper, legal rental agreement digitally for all tenants via their Resident Portal. The standard initial lease is for 6 months and can easily be renewed.

What are the timings and rules?

Gate closes securely by 10:00 – 10:30 PM depending on the property specific guidelines. Visitors are only allowed till 8 PM in common areas for safety. Smoking and loud noise environments inside rooms are strictly restricted to maintain study conditions.

How do I pay rent?

Rent can be effortlessly paid via UPI, Net Banking, or card via your exclusive Resident Dashboard. You will receive automated WhatsApp reminders for seamless logging and can download real-time digital invoices immediately after payment.
