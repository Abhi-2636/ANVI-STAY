About Anvi Stay

Who We Are

Premium student housing, reimagined for the modern learner.

Anvi Stay is a premium student housing company based near Lovely Professional University (LPU) in Phagwara, Punjab. Founded with a mission to redefine student accommodation, we manage 90+ rooms across 9 properties — offering fully furnished, safe, and modern living spaces at affordable prices.

Our tagline "We Listen, We Care, You Stay" reflects our commitment to student satisfaction. From 24/7 support and automated rent collection to digital agreements and proactive maintenance — we leverage technology to ensure a hassle-free living experience. Whether you’re a domestic or international student, Anvi Stay is your trusted home away from home.
